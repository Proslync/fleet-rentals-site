FAQ

Quick answers.

Is insurance included?

Trips booked through our Turo listings include Turo's protection plans. Direct weekly/monthly rentals require proof that your own auto policy extends to rental vehicles (most do) — we verify before pickup, for both of our protection.

Do you deliver to the airports?

Yes — Dulles (IAD) and Reagan National (DCA), plus hotels, offices, and Metro stations across the DC metro. Delivery fees depend on location.

What's the security deposit?

A refundable card hold, released after the vehicle comes back in the condition it left. Amount depends on vehicle and rental length — stated clearly in your quote before you commit to anything.

Is there a mileage limit?

Generous daily allowances are included, with a simple per-mile rate beyond that — all in writing in your agreement. No surprises.

Do you offer chauffeured service?

Not currently — all rentals are self-drive. If that changes, it will be announced here first.
