Renter requirements

We keep it simple, but we do verify. Every direct rental requires:

  • 1. Valid driver's license, age 25+
  • 2. Proof of personal auto insurance that extends to rentals
  • 3. Signed rental agreement before keys
  • 4. Refundable security deposit (card hold)

These protect both of us — and they're why our trucks stay in the condition you're renting them for.

FAQ

Quick answers.

Is insurance included?

Trips booked through our Turo listings include Turo's protection plans. Direct weekly/monthly rentals require proof that your own auto policy extends to rental vehicles (most do) — we verify this before pickup, for both of our protection.

Do you deliver to the airports?

Yes — Dulles (IAD) and Reagan National (DCA), plus hotels, offices, and Metro stations across the DC metro. Delivery fees depend on location.

What's the security deposit?

A refundable card hold, released after the vehicle is returned in the condition it left. Amount depends on vehicle and rental length — stated clearly in your quote before you commit to anything.

Is there a mileage limit?

Generous daily allowances are included, with a simple per-mile rate beyond that — all in writing in your agreement, no surprises.

Do you offer chauffeured service?

Not currently — all rentals are self-drive. If that changes, it will be announced here first.
